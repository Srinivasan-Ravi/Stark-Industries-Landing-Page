About Us

Stark Industries became the world leader in the development of manufacturing advanced weapons. Stark Industries quickly branched out into other scientific fields including aeronautics, robotics, micro-technology and fringe science.

CEO

Stark is initially depicted as an industrialist, genius inventor, and former playboy who is CEO of Stark Industries. Initially the chief weapons manufacturer for the U.S. military, he has a change of heart and redirects his technical knowledge into creating mechanized suits of armor, which he uses to defend Earth.

Products

Stark Industries gave us the Quinjets, the War Machine suit, and of course the infamous, Iron Man suit. But did you know that Stark Industries dates back to the 19th century? They've also made the coolest jet-powered roller skates! It truly makes the most innovative and awesome tech in the world.
